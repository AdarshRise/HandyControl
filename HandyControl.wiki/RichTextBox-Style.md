This just replaces the standard RichTextBox when you drop in the library, nothing fancy to activate it. 

## Usage
```
 <RichTextBox>
            <FlowDocument>
                <Paragraph>
                    <Run FontSize="20" Text="Title"/>
                </Paragraph>
                <Paragraph>
                    <Run Text="Text"/>
                </Paragraph>
                <Paragraph>
                    <Hyperlink>https://github.com/NaBian/HandyControl</Hyperlink>
                </Paragraph>
            </FlowDocument>
        </RichTextBox>
```
