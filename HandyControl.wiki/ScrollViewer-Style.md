This just replaces the standard ScrollViewer when you drop in the library, nothing fancy to activate it. 

## Usage
```
<ScrollViewer HorizontalScrollBarVisibility="Auto" VerticalScrollBarVisibility="Hidden">
 
</ScrollViewer>
```
